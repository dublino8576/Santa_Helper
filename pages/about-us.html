About The Project

Helping users create, organise, and share their Christmas wish lists to make the holiday season joyful and stress-free.

Our Mission

The Christmas Tree project was created to make holiday planning simple, joyful, and stress-free. Our goal is to help users organise their Christmas wishes, count down to the big day, and find inspiration all in one festive, easy-to-use platform.
